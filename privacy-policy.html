Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Feb 09, 2026

At The Chess Gurukul, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through our website, especially regarding our enrollment forms and educational services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you interact with our website. This occurs when you fill out an enquiry or enrollment form, or contact us via WhatsApp/Email. This information may include:

  • Parent/Guardian name and contact details (Email, Phone).
  • Student's name and age for class placement.
  • Details regarding previous chess experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used strictly for educational and administrative purposes, including:

  • Responding to your program enquiries.
  • Processing enrollments and coordinating class schedules.
  • Communicating educational progress and updates.
  • Improving our website and measuring marketing effectiveness.

3. Cookies and Google AdSense

This website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. We also use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses the DART cookie to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out by visiting the Google Ad Privacy Policy.

4. Data Protection and Security

We value your trust. We implement security measures to protect your data and strictly do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Parental Consent and Children’s Data

As a chess academy for kids, we prioritize children's safety. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without Parental Consent. All student data must be provided by a parent or legal guardian. Parents may contact us at any time to review or delete their child’s information.

6. Consent

By using our website and submitting your details through our forms, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
