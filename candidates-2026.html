The tournament runs from March 28 to April 16, 2026, and follows a double round-robin format. That means:

  • Each of the 8 players faces every other player twice.
  • Once with the White pieces and once with the Black pieces.

Consistency is crucial. If players are tied for first place after 14 rounds, rapid and blitz tie-break games will determine the challenger.
